## Business context (not design directives — for awareness)

- Cross-sell/upsell to existing customers is a strategic priority: "Exposing our clients to current products of Prism is becoming an even more important thing"
- Modular pricing to enter previously excluded market segments (1-year, 2-year programs that couldn't afford all-inclusive Prism)
- "We have to say okay buy what you want to buy pay for what you want but eventually buy more from us"
- Module → user persona mapping: Director of Clinical Education ↔ clinical products; Director of Academic Education ↔ Exam Management. Same faculty team, different roles within it
- Exact One (hospitals) proving harder than anticipated; doubling down on school ecosystem
