Program Director

Two gates before faculty see anything.

The instant a survey closes it goes to "Pending admin review." Grades must post to SIS first. Then admin moderates comments, hide unprofessional ones, never edit. Then publish. Watermark and CourseEval do this. We do it better, with the gates explicit.
