## How to add a persona

## What NOT to do

- Don't invent personas based on product name guesses ("checklist" implies clinical skills tracking — but who uses it, when, and why is not yet decided).
- Don't copy-paste personas from other Exxat products. Skills Checklist's user model may differ from PCE / Exam Management / Patient Log.
- Don't write aspirational personas. Write real personas confirmed by user research, customer interviews, or stakeholder decisions.
